Godzilla X Kong: The New Empire

In Theaters March 29,2024.

Five years after an explosive epic showdown between Godzilla vs Kong and after defeating the mechanical foe MechaGodzilla.

But now in this new cinematic adventure entry in the Monsterverse a new undiscovered threat from Hallow Earth is bringing war to our world, challenging for the very existence of everything. To save our world as we know it Godzilla and Kong must put aside their rivalry and work together to stop to this new threat from bringing war. The new installment will dive deeper into the histories of these Titans, their origins and the mysteries of Skull Island and beyond, while uncovering the mythic battle that helped forge these extraordinary beings and tied them to humankind forever.
