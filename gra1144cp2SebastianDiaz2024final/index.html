Ingredients

The list of ingredients to use for Strawberry Sando is

  • A bag of bread
  • A box of Strawberries
  • An Electric Whisk (to mix)
  • Sugar (white or brown optional)
  • A plastic wrap
  • And a carton of Heavy whipping cream

Instruction steps

The cooking procedure is very simple. The following starts with.....

  1. A large bowl and add 2 cups of heavy whipping cream
  2. Add 4 tablespoons of sugar
  3. Whisk and mix until it’s thick
  4. Get a slice of bread and cut the crust
  5. Add a layer of whipping cream
  6. Wash the strawberries and put in 5 strawberries
  7. Put the strawberries in a diagonal position
  8. Add another layer of whipping cream on top of Strawberries
  9. Topped it with another slice of bread
  10. Then wrapped the Sando in plastic wrap
  11. Place the Sando in the fridge for 30 minutes or an hour
  12. Then cut in half the Sando and enjoy eating
